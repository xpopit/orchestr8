## üí´ Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders of the **orchestr8** community pledge to make participation in our project and community a **harassment-free experience for everyone**, regardless of:

- Age, body size, visible or invisible disability
- Ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression
- Level of experience, education, socio-economic status
- Nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion
- Sexual identity and orientation

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an **open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community** focused on advancing enterprise-grade autonomous software orchestration.

## üö® Reporting Violations

### How to Report

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it:

#### Primary Methods

1. **GitHub Discussions** (Preferred for non-sensitive issues)
   - [Create Private Discussion](https://github.com/seth-schultz/orchestr8/discussions)
   - Tag maintainers: @seth-schultz

2. **Email** (For sensitive reports)
   - contact@orchestr8.builders
   - Reports handled confidentially

#### What to Include

A complete report should include:

```
- Your contact information
- Names (real, usernames, or pseudonyms) of individuals involved
- Description of the incident (dates, locations, context)
- Supporting documentation (screenshots, links, logs)
- Any additional context that may be helpful
```

**Template:**

```markdown
## Code of Conduct Violation Report

**Reporter**: [Your name or anonymous]
**Date**: [Date of incident]
**Location**: [GitHub issue #123, PR #456, etc.]

### Individuals Involved
- [List names/usernames]

### Description
[Detailed description of what happened]

### Supporting Evidence
- [Screenshot 1]
- [Link to comment]
- [Other evidence]

### Impact
[How this affected you or the community]
```

## ‚öñÔ∏è Enforcement Guidelines

Community leaders follow these **Community Impact Guidelines**:

### 1. üìù Correction

**Community Impact**: Minor violation (inappropriate language, unprofessional behavior)

**Consequence**:
- Private written warning
- Clarification of why the behavior was inappropriate
- Public apology may be requested

**Example**: Using inappropriate language in a GitHub comment

---

### 2. ‚ö†Ô∏è Warning

**Community Impact**: Single incident or series of violations

**Consequence**:
- Warning with consequences for continued behavior
- No interaction with people involved for specified period
- Includes avoiding community spaces and external channels
- Violating terms may lead to temporary or permanent ban

**Example**: Repeated trolling or disruptive comments

---

### 3. üö´ Temporary Ban

**Community Impact**: Serious violation of community standards

**Consequence**:
- Temporary ban from all interaction (specified period)
- No public or private interaction with people involved
- No interaction with those enforcing Code of Conduct
- Violating terms may lead to permanent ban

**Example**: Sustained inappropriate behavior, harassment

**Duration**: 7 days to 90 days (based on severity)

---

### 4. üî¥ Permanent Ban

**Community Impact**: Pattern of violations, sustained harassment, aggression toward classes of individuals

**Consequence**:
- Permanent ban from all public interaction
- Removal of all contributions (at maintainers' discretion)
- No path to reinstatement

**Example**: Sexual harassment, threats, malicious code injection
